Unofficial experimental fork (shomanjk/sms-backup-plus). Not affiliated with jberkel or henrichg. Not ready for daily use — WorkManager has replaced JobDispatcher; confirm auto-backup on your phone (especially RCS / OEM battery limits) before relying on it.

Upstream author: Jan Berkel (and many others, see credits below)

Automatically backs up your SMS/MMS and call logs to Gmail or any other IMAP server. Can also restore SMS / call logs after they have been backed up.

New in this release:

  • 0.2.0 — WorkManager replaces JobDispatcher. Incoming ContentUriTrigger watches SMS and MMS; OEM network-constraint workaround so background jobs are not stuck waiting on false CONNECTIVITY. Sync log and regular reschedule fixed for WorkManager runs.
  • 0.1.4 — Prompt for Receive SMS/MMS when automatic backup is enabled so incoming messages can trigger backups.
  • 0.1.3 — Faster/more reliable IMAP to Gmail-style hosts (TLS SNI without blocked reflection; prefer IPv4 before broken IPv6 timeouts). Fixed toolbar and Backup/Restore under system bars on Android 15. Removed broken in-app Donate on this sideload fork.
  • 0.1.2 — Short Welcome / What's new dialogs on first launch and upgrades; full About page (menu) remains for credits and docs.
  • 0.1.1 — Fixed crash on startup on Android 12+ (API 31+) by forcing the AlarmManager scheduler before JobDispatcher PendingIntents without mutability flags.
  • 0.1.0 — Experimental maintained fork (Android 7+ trigger ports; not daily-use yet)

See CHANGELOG.md on GitHub for the full list. Upstream history is archived in CHANGES.

Privacy Policy

To function properly SMS Backup+ needs to have full access to various types of private data stored on your phone as well as your email account. Take a moment to read the Privacy Policy (upstream; this fork has not published a separate policy).
